Our story

Built on 10 years of family industry experience.

I grew up working in my family's duct cleaning business in Connecticut — loading equipment, running jobs, learning every corner of the trade from my father, who has spent 25+ years in the industry. Jax Dryer Vent Cleaners exists to be different — a specialist who shows up on time, does measurable work, and stands behind every job with documentation. Jacksonville deserves that.

How we work

Three things we never compromise on.

We measure everything

Every job includes a CFM airflow reading before we start and after we finish. The difference in those numbers is proof of the work we did. No other Jacksonville competitor does this consistently.

We show up when we say we will

Two-hour arrival windows, text confirmation when we're on the way, and a marked van so you know exactly who is at your door. Reliability is the most basic promise a service company can make — and the most commonly broken one.

We tell you what we find, honestly

If your vent is fine and doesn't need extra work, we'll tell you that too. If there's a problem, we explain it clearly, show you the evidence, and give you options — no pressure, no upsell theater.
