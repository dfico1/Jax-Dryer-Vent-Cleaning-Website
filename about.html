Our story

Built on over 25 years of family industry experience.

I grew up working in my family's duct and dryer vent cleaning business in Connecticut — loading equipment, running jobs, and learning every corner of the trade from my father who has spent 25+ years in the industry. By the time I moved to Jacksonville, I had over a decade of hands-on experience.

When I looked at the Jacksonville market, I saw something clear: plenty of HVAC companies and handymen who clean dryer vents as a side job, and nobody who actually specializes in it. No one offering before-and-after airflow measurements. No one who could diagnose between a problematic dryer vent or a bad dryer. Just a race to the bottom on price.

Jax Dryer Vent Cleaning LLC exists to be different. We are specialists who show up on time, perform measurable work, and stand behind every job with documentation. Northeast Florida deserves this level of service.

At the end of the day, we're more than just a service provider, we're your neighbors. As a family-owned local business, we believe in treating every home with the same care and respect we'd give our own. Our reputation is built on trust, and we're committed to doing right by the community we live in. That means honest pricing, no high-pressure upsells, and a promise that if we can't make your home safer, we won't charge you a dime.

— Dan, Owner

How we work

Three things we never compromise on.

We measure everything

Every job includes a CFM airflow reading before we start and after we finish. The difference in those numbers is proof of the work we did.

We show up when we say we will

Arrival windows communicated in advance, text confirmation when we're on the way, and a marked van so you know exactly who is at your door.

We tell you what we find, honestly

If your vent is fine and doesn't need extra work, we'll tell you that too. If there's a problem, we explain it clearly, show you the evidence, and give you options. No pressure, no upsell.
