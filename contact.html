Common questions

Before you call — quick answers.

How long does a dryer vent cleaning take?

Most residential jobs take 45–75 minutes from arrival to departure. Repairs may take longer — we'll let you know upfront.

How often should I clean my dryer vent?

At least once per year for most homes. Jacksonville's high humidity means lint compacts faster — homes with long vent runs or heavy dryer use benefit from twice-yearly service.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Yes — we need access to your laundry room and often to the exterior vent termination. We'll communicate your arrival window ahead of time, show up when we say we will, and text you when we're on the way.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit card, debit card, check, Venmo, and Zelle. Digital invoice sent immediately after the job is complete.

Do you service condos and apartment buildings?

Yes — including buildings with booster fans and longer shared vent runs. We work with property managers and HOA boards on recurring service contracts throughout Jacksonville.

What signs mean my dryer vent needs cleaning?

Clothes taking more than one cycle to dry, the dryer running hot to the touch, a burning smell during operation, or it's been more than a year since your last cleaning.
