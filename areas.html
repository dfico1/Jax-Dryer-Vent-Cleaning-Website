Jacksonville-specific knowledge

Why Jacksonville homes need extra attention.

High humidity accelerates lint buildup

Jacksonville's year-round humidity causes lint to compact more densely than in dry climates. What needs annual cleaning in Connecticut may need twice-yearly service here in Florida.

Newer construction = longer vent runs

Jacksonville's new developments (Nocatee, Ponte Vedra, St. Johns) feature slab-on-grade construction with longer vent runs — often 25–50 ft — that require extended brush reach.

Rooftop terminations are common here

Many Jacksonville homes vent through the roof rather than through a wall. These terminations require safe ladder access and specific cleaning techniques that most companies skip.
