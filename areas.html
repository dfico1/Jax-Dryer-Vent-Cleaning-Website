Jacksonville-specific knowledge

Why Jacksonville homes need extra attention.

High humidity accelerates lint buildup

Jacksonville's year-round humidity causes lint to compact more densely than in dry climates. What needs annual cleaning in other states may need twice-yearly service here in Florida.

Newer construction = longer vent runs

Jacksonville's new developments (Nocatee, Ponte Vedra, St. Johns) feature slab-on-grade construction with longer vent runs, often greater than 25 ft, requiring extended reach.
